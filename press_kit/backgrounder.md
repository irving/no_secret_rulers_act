### Historical Context

- **1919**: After a stroke, President Wilson’s wife, Edith, made presidential decisions in secret.
- **2024**: Representative Kay Granger reportedly moved into a senior care facility without informing colleagues, constituents, or the public. 
- **Recent**: Allegations persist that President Biden was too cognitively impaired to have performed his duties.

### What the NSRA Would Change

- **Mandatory reporting** of incapacitation
- **Clear legal responsibility** for disclosure of substitute decision-makers
- **Criminal penalties** for secret governance
- **Whistleblower protections** (and penalties for malicious false reporting)
